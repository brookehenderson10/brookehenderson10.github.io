What We Do

We are a coalition of student groups and community members who came together because we think that there should be free tampons and pads provided on campus in central locations. We are composed by the the National Women’s Liberation- Gainesville Chapter, Planned Parenthood Generation Action, Pride Student Union, and the Women’s Student Association.

We’re collecting signatures to prove to UF Faculty and Administration that this is an issue that matters and needs to be dealt with. As the top 9 poblic insitution in the country, we should be leading by example, but the map below tells another story.
