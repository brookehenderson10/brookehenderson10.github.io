Brooke Henderson

Brooke Henderson is a multimedia reporter from Pompano Beach, Fla. dedicated to covering overlooked communities. As an Ida B. Wells Scholar, Brooke has been covering racial inequities in education including highschool test prep access. Prior to J-School she has covered a wide range of topics from LGBTQ issues to legislative changes in agriculture including hive theft among Florida bee farmers. She is particularly interested in telling stories about how government money moves through the prison system. She has interned at the Miami Herald and The Miami Times among other news outlets. She is a member of NABJ.
